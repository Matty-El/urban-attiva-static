Our Team

Jane

The so-called Boss

She may be the head of Urban Attiva, but deep down she knows she's really the obedient servant to Percy, Aggie and Daisy Dog. Jane doesn’t mind though because there’s no better team to work alongside. She is a keen animal conservationist and volunteer at her local cat adoption centre.

Percy

Likes to make his views heard

The most vocal member of the team, Percy likes to tell us when he’s not happy and it’s probably because he’s hangry and wants his breakfast or dinner. He can usually be found shouting at delivery drivers and magpies, or lying upside down with his Attiva in the air.

Aggie

The brains of the outfit

She may be short in stature but she is a force to be reckoned with. Aggie can usually be found on the top level of the cat tree where she is ideally placed to tap the shoulder of anyone that may try and sneak past without a food offering or a face rub. Your reward is a chirp of delight.

Daisy Dog

The eternal optimist

Daisy’s contribution to the team lies mainly in quality control, here she excels at running around in mud to thoroughly test our products. Daisy can be found wherever there is a fetid pond or puddle to play in, or at the slightest crinkle of a food wrapper.

What we do

Urban Attiva is a family run business dedicated to providing only the very best sports and outdoor equipment. Our aim is to showcase carefully chosen, high quality products that we ourselves use. We partner with events such as The Humber Swim.

Our Story

The idea for Urban Attiva was conceived by Jane and Matt during the Coronavirus pandemic. We both love cycling and wild swimming and Matt is also a keen long distance trail runner.

With a love of outdoor sports we also recognise the benefits of indoor training.

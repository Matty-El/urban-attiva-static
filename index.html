What we do

Urban Paws is a family run business dedicated to provide only the very best for your pets. Our aim is to showcase carefully chosen, high quality, British designed or British made pet products alongside our very own creations. Our ethos is to only sell you products that we would give to our own pets. This way you can buy with total confidence.

PADD Suit

The PADD suit was envisioned by Jane after spending a very long summer making countless visits to our vet and vet referral specialists with one of our cats. Spending so much time surrounded by poorly pet patients inspired her to create the PADD suit. If you’ve visited our About Us page you may have worked out that the the PADD suit is in fact dedicated to our three amazing pets, Percy, Aggie and Daisy Dog.

The PADD suit has been masterminded by us and our British designers, along with valuable input from our veterinary surgeons. It is a comfortable and sustainable alternative to the Elizabethan collar, or 'cone of shame’, made from 4 way stretch bamboo fabric, which itself provides natural anti-bacterial properties, ideal for helping wounds and surgery scars heal. Bamboo is a highly sustainable material, which not only makes it better for the environment but it is also an incredibly soft, comfortable fabric that can be worn all day.
